Furniture Cleaning

Furniture in your home has a tendency to attract just about any odor and any body oil from you, your children, or your animals. Using a special low-moisture and low-temp system and a variation of cleaning agents, we perform a deep clean on just about any furniture in your home. We have the ability to clean any upholstered piece of furniture and even have a process to clean and protect leather furniture. For upholstered furniture we offer a fabric protectant to fight off body oils and dirt. Once we finish your furniture it will be cleaner, healthier, and smelling like new.
