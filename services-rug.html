Area Rug Cleaning

We can clean almost any type of area rug fiber. Our special techniques of dye-locking and chemical variations make us the pinnacle of area rug cleaners in Northwest Arkansas. At your convenience we will pick up your rug(s) and bring them back to our shop where we will take the utmost care in cleaning them to ensure that none of the colors bleed and to give them a thorough cleaning. As with carpet cleaning, we offer pet odor/urine treatments as well as carpet protectant to keep your rug clean longer. After cleaning, we use air movers to dry your rugs and then return them to you.
