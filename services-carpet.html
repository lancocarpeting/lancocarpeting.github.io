Carpet Cleaning

We use a van-mounted carpet cleaning machine with built in heating mechanisms. During our cleaning process we thoroughly vacuum your carpet, then pre-treat it with an "eco-friendly" agent to break down the soils. Depending on the amount of soil in the carpet, we will pre-agitate the carpet with a rotary machine to ensure that all the soil becomes loose enough to extract out. Finally, we use our carpet cleaning machine to steam clean your carpet with 200-450 pounds of water pressure to ensure a deep clean. The machine's vacuuming system then extracts the water and soil leaving your carpet clean, soft, and smelling good. We also have special treatments for pet odor/urine issues and carpet protectant options. For your convenience we also offer air movers to expedite the drying of your carpet.
